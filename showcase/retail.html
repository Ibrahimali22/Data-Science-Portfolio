SQL Magic (Window Functions)

Implementing MoM Growth tracking via LAG and OVER:


WITH MonthlySales AS (
  SELECT strftime('%Y-%m', OrderDate) AS Month, SUM(Revenue) AS Revenue 
  FROM Orders GROUP BY 1
)
SELECT Month, Revenue,
       ROUND(((Revenue - LAG(Revenue) OVER (ORDER BY Month)) / LAG(Revenue) OVER (ORDER BY Month)) * 100, 2) AS GrowthPct
FROM MonthlySales;
